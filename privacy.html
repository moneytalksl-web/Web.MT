MoneyTalks Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2023

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how MoneyTalks collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

Data Type What We Collect Why We Collect It
Account Data Email address, username Account creation and verification
Usage Data Pages visited, clicks, preferences Improve user experience
Browser Data LocalStorage data (balance, settings) Remember your preferences
Payment Data Processed by PayPal (we don't see card details) Plan purchases

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide Services: To operate and maintain MoneyTalks
  • Communication: Send verification emails and updates
  • Improvement: Analyze usage to improve our services
  • Security: Protect against fraud and abuse
  • Legal Compliance: Meet legal obligations

3. Data Storage & Security

Storage Location: Your data is stored in your browser's LocalStorage and temporary server sessions.

Security Measures: We use standard security practices, but no online service is 100% secure.

Data Retention: We retain data as long as your account is active or as needed for legal purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

We use these trusted services:

  • PayPal: For secure payment processing
  • EmailJS: For sending verification emails
  • Google Fonts: For typography
  • YouTube: Embedded videos

Each service has its own privacy policy. We recommend reviewing them.

5. Cookies & LocalStorage

We use browser LocalStorage to:

  • Remember your login status
  • Save your theme preference
  • Store your balance and plan information
  • Track referral links

You can clear this data anytime via browser settings.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request what data we have
  • Correction: Correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request data deletion
  • Opt-out: Stop marketing communications

To exercise these rights, email: themoneytalks.web@gmail.com

7. Children's Privacy

MoneyTalks is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. International Transfers

Our services are hosted globally. Your data may be processed outside your country.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy. We'll notify you of significant changes via email or site notice.

10. Contact Us

For privacy questions or concerns:

Email: themoneytalks.web@gmail.com

Response Time: We aim to respond within 7 business days.
